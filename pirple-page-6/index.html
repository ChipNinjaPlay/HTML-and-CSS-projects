Description:

Milk cake is one of my favorites because it's taste and well combining with coffe, it's main ingredient is milk and don't use yeast.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs, room temperature.
  • 2 cups sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 2-1/4 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 2-1/4 teaspoons baking powder.
  • 1-1/4 cups 2% milk.
  • 10 tablespoons butter, cubed.

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degüî•.
  2. In a large bowl, beat eggs on high speed for 5 minutes or until thick and lemon-coloredüçã.
  3. Gradually add sugar, beating until mixture is light and fluffyü•£.
  4. Beat in vanilla. Combine flour and baking powder.
  5. gradually add to batter beating on low speed until smooth.
  6. In a small saucepan, heat milk and butter just until butter is meltedü•õ.
  7. Gradually add to batter; beat just until combined.
  8. Pour into a greased 13x9-in. baking pan.
  9. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 30-35 minutes.
  10. Cool on a wire rack ‚ô®Ô∏è.
